Moongfali Poha

Moongfali Poha is an amazing Indian breakfast dish that is perfect
for those who like to start their day with a punch of flavors.
This dish is made with flattened rice flakes or poha, mixed with peanuts,
onions, green chilies, and a variety of spices that add a zesty and savory
taste to the meal. The roasted peanuts in the poha lend a delicious nutty
crunch, while the onions and green chilies add a bit of tang and spice.
You don't have to spend hours in the kitchen to make this dish;
it's a quick and easy recipe that you can prepare in no time.

Ingredients

  • Flattened rice flakes or poha (1 cup)
  • Roasted peanuts (1/2 cup)
  • One medium-sized onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon of mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice flakes or poha with water and soak in the water for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Drain the water completely and set the poha aside.
  3. Heat a tablespoon of oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds and 1/2 teaspoon of cumin seeds.
  5. Let them cook until they start to splutter.
  6. Sauté one finely chopped onion until it's light brown.
  7. Add 2-3 finely chopped green chilies and sauté for a few seconds.
  8. Stir in 1/2 cup of roasted peanuts and mix well with the onion and green chilies.
  9. Season with 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric powder and salt to taste, and stir to combine.
  10. Add the soaked and drained poha and mix well with the other ingredients.
  11. Cook for 5-7 minutes on low heat, stirring occasionally, until the poha becomes soft.
  12. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and enjoy
