Moongfali Poha

Moongfali Poha is an amazing Indian breakfast dish that is perfect
for those who like to start their day with a punch of flavors.
This dish is made with flattened rice flakes or poha, mixed with peanuts,
onions, green chilies, and a variety of spices that add a zesty and savory
taste to the meal. The roasted peanuts in the poha lend a delicious nutty
crunch, while the onions and green chilies add a bit of tang and spice.
You don't have to spend hours in the kitchen to make this dish;
it's a quick and easy recipe that you can prepare in no time.
